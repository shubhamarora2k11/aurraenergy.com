Tubular Plate

Batteries made from tubular plates have the following characteristics:

1. High Electrical Performance (up To 20% More Than Flat Plates).

2. Higher Life Respect To Flat Plates.

3. No Active Material Shedding (which Shortens Cell Life).

4. Tubular Battery Plates Have More Exposed Surface Area Than Plain Flat Plates. 

  5. Tubular Batteries Last Longer, For 3/4 Years Under Proper Maintenance Condition.

6. Highly Reliable Compared To Normal Flat Plate Batteries.

7. The Spine Of Tubular Batteries Are Made Using High Pressure Casting Method Which Ensures Long Life, Even Under Heavy Temperature And Rough Usage.

8. Perform Consistently Under Any Conditions Hence Suitable For Sensitive And Heavy Applications

9. Low Maintenance – No Need To Top-up With Distilled Water Frequently (depending On Use)

10. Long Standby Life Compared To Flat Plate Batteries
